Choose your desired brainwave frequency for optimal mental state

What are Binaural Beats?

Binaural beats are a form of auditory illusion where two tones of slightly different frequencies are presented separately to each ear. The brain perceives a third tone ‚Äî the binaural beat ‚Äî which is the difference between the two frequencies.


How it works: These beats are believed to influence brainwave activity and may support various mental states like relaxation, focus, meditation, and sleep through natural "brainwave entrainment."

Session Preparation

  • Use headphones: Required for proper binaural effect
  • Set volume: Comfortable listening level (not too loud)
  • Safety: Stop if you feel any discomfort

Moving Focus Training

Improve your sustained attention and visual tracking skills

Focus Training

GREEN DOT - Focus Target
BLUE DOT - Distractor
RED DOT - Distractor
YELLOW DOT - Distractor
PURPLE DOT - Distractor
1
Focus ONLY on the GREEN dot: Keep your eyes locked on the moving green dot at all times. This is your primary target and trains your smooth pursuit eye movements.
2
Ignore ALL distractor dots: The BLUE, RED, YELLOW, and PURPLE moving dots are designed to pull your attention away from the green target. Resist the urge to look at them.
3
Maintain smooth tracking: Follow the green dot with smooth, continuous eye movements. Avoid jerky movements and keep your head still.
4
Build distraction resistance: When you notice your attention being pulled to any colored distractor dots, gently redirect your focus back to the green dot.
üß† Evidence-Based Benefits:

Training with visual distractors significantly enhances selective attention, cognitive control, and the ability to filter irrelevant information. This strengthens the brain's executive control networks.

Body Scan Meditation

What is Body Scan Meditation?

A simple yet powerful mindfulness practice where you systematically focus on different parts of your body. This evidence-based technique promotes deep relaxation, reduces stress, and builds greater body awareness.

Reduces Stress & Cortisol

Just 20 minutes daily significantly lowers cortisol - your body's main stress hormone linked to inflammation and sleep issues.

8-week study shows measurable cortisol reduction (Schultchen et al., 2019)

Manages Chronic Pain

Changes your relationship with pain through increased awareness and acceptance. Recommended by Jon Kabat-Zinn as the most effective meditation for pain relief.

Review of 13 studies confirms chronic pain reduction (2017)

Improves Sleep Quality

Activates your body's relaxation response, leading to better sleep and reduced daytime fatigue.

Clinical trials confirm improved sleep quality (Black et al., 2015)

Enhances Emotional Regulation

Builds your ability to recognize and respond to internal body signals, leading to better emotional control and reduced anxiety.

8-week study shows improved emotional awareness (Fischer et al., 2017)

Improves Focus & Attention

Trains your concentration skills, helping you stay present and reducing mind-wandering.

Studies show enhanced attention through brain changes (Tang et al., 2015)

Releases Physical Tension

Helps identify and release physical tension you didn't know you were carrying, breaking the stress cycle.

Regular practice reduces muscle tension and inflammation
